About

Statepedia is an interactive encyclopedia for all 50 U.S. states. Explore 16 categories of data including population, economy, education, crime, health, politics, sports, tourism, and more — all from one beautifully designed app.

FAQ

Where does the data come from?
Data is sourced from the U.S. Census Bureau, Bureau of Economic Analysis, Bureau of Labor Statistics, FBI, CDC, USDA, National Park Service, and other public sources.

Is Statepedia free?
Yes. Statepedia is completely free with no ads or in-app purchases.

How often is the data updated?
Data is updated with each app release as new government data becomes available.
