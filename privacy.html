Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Overview

Statepedia ("the App") is designed with your privacy in mind. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Collection

Statepedia does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected (name, email, location, etc.)
  • No usage analytics or tracking is performed
  • No cookies or similar technologies are used
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • No account creation is required

Data Sources

All data displayed in the App comes from publicly available U.S. government sources including the Census Bureau, Bureau of Economic Analysis, Bureau of Labor Statistics, FBI, CDC, USDA, and National Park Service. This data is bundled within the App and does not require an internet connection to access.

Third-Party Services

Statepedia does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Statepedia does not collect any data from any user, including children under 13. The App is safe and appropriate for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date.
